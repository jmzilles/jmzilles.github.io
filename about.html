Jackson Zilles
Projects About

Jackson is an inventive engineer originally inspired by additive manufacturing, problem-solving, and designing for solutions. He likes to find annoyances and problems in our designed world and question why they exist, how they came to be, and how they could be remedied.

Jackson earned his degree in mechanical engineering at the University of California, Berkeley with a focus on design and entrepreneurship.

Previously, Jackson has worked in mechanical and mechtronics design for vehicle seating systems, as well as in R&D engineering designing surgical devices. He has also worked in consulting supporting the development of medical device test assemblies.

Outside of engineering, Jackson enjoys surfing and being around the ocean, traveling to new countries, and motorcycles.
