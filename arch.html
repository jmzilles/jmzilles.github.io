This project was started with Arch Day Design, and engineering consulting company in Ventura, CA, in July of 2020. The client wanted a redesign of their current model of a medical device, a cup which regulated the amount of liquid dispensed per sip. This device was created to help those with difficulty swallowing, preventing choking and drownings.

As an engineering intern at Arch Day, I ran the prototyping process of this medical device. I used personal 3D printers to create high-quality models that were shipped across the US for testing. I worked with multiple variations of the model, suggesting design changes to better fit the clients needs and aid the printing process.
